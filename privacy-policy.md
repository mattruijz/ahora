# Ahora — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 24, 2026

Ahora is a Chrome Extension that helps you pause before online purchases. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Ahora collects, how it is stored, and how you can control it.

## What data does Ahora collect?

Ahora stores the following data **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`):

- **Your decisions**: When you interact with the Ahora overlay (decline, postpone, or purchase), we save the website domain, your choice, the estimated cart amount, and a timestamp.
- **Aggregated stats**: Total amount not spent, number of conscious choices, and your streak of consecutive days.
- **Your preferences**: Selected theme (Default, Forest, or Calm) and analytics opt-in preference.

## Where is the data stored?

All data is stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's storage API.

**Optional anonymous analytics (opt-in only):** If you choose to enable "Share anonymous usage data" in the dashboard, Ahora sends anonymous usage events (such as when the overlay is shown and which choice you made) to Google Analytics via the Measurement Protocol. This data includes:
- The website domain (e.g. `bol.com`)
- Your choice (decline, postpone, or purchase)
- Your selected theme
- The extension version
- A randomly generated anonymous ID (UUID) that identifies your installation — not you personally

This ID is generated locally, stored in `chrome.storage.local`, and contains no personal information. You can opt out at any time using the toggle in the dashboard, and resetting your data also removes the anonymous ID.

## Does Ahora track me?

Ahora never tracks you without your consent. Ahora does not:
- Collect personal information (name, email, IP address)
- Use cookies or tracking pixels
- Monitor your browsing activity outside of checkout pages
- Access your payment information or cart contents beyond the displayed total

If you opt in to anonymous analytics, limited anonymous usage data is sent to Google Analytics (see above). This is strictly opt-in and off by default.

## What websites does Ahora access?

Ahora activates only on checkout and cart pages of supported online stores (such as Amazon, Bol.com, Zalando, and others listed in the extension). It detects checkout pages using URL patterns and page structure — it does not read or store the content of these pages.

## How can I delete my data?

You can delete all Ahora data at any time:
1. Click the Ahora icon in your Chrome toolbar
2. Scroll to the bottom of the dashboard
3. Click **"Reset data"** and confirm

Alternatively, uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

## Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension's update notes.
